    Introduction PF0

    Welcome to our Python Fundamentals course. Starting out in coding can be quite daunting for newcomers, as they often might not know where to start. This conundrum begins even before you start to code, taking the shape of getting set up, jargon and terminology, and being faced with the myriad of tools, packages and editors available to you.

    With this short (fully optional), reference resource we hope to provide some details about Python and getting started as a coder, that should help you to focus your understanding and prepare you for the non-coding aspects of Python that are still fundamental to becoming a programmer.

    We must stress that this resource is optional, and considered a reference material. Rather than memorising its contents, we hope this will be a launchpad for your coding journey, and a useful material to dip back into, when things get a little hazy or rusty, going forward.
